COMMON QUESTIONS

Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in your tour packages?

Our packages typically include accommodation, transportation, guided tours, and select meals as specified in each package description. Some packages also include special activities and entrance fees to attractions.

How do I book a package?

You can book a package directly through our website by clicking the "View Details" button on any package, then following the booking steps. Alternatively, you can contact our travel experts for assistance.

Can I customize an existing package?

Yes! Most of our packages can be customized to suit your preferences. Contact our travel experts to discuss your specific requirements.

What is your cancellation policy?

Our standard policy allows for full refunds up to 30 days before departure, 50% refund for cancellations 15-29 days before departure, and no refunds for cancellations less than 15 days before departure. We recommend travel insurance for all bookings.
